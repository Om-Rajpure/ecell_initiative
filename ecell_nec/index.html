Guidelines

  • NEC is a 5-6 month-long challenge focusing on building and fostering your own Entrepreneurship Cell.
  • Teams who are participating for the first time and do not have an E-Cell in their college are advised to register in the BASIC track.
  • It is mandatory for the top 10 Teams (BASIC) of previous years to participate in the ADVANCED track this year.
  • Teams who took part in the ADVANCED track last year are not allowed to register in the BASIC track.
  • Registration deadline is 27th July 2025, though teams can register later on keeping the following points in mind:
  • Teams registering before the registration deadline can take advantage of the early bird bonus, that can provide them with some extra points.
  • The window for each task submission will close on 18th October 2025. Till then you can submit any task.
  • This may also delay the mentor allotment for your team. The mentor will only be allotted when you submit the Preliminary Task.

What is NEC ?

Entrepreneurship Cell is essential for any college because it develops students' entrepreneurial spirit, which we believe is instrumental for our country to develop. NEC is the platform that helps colleges build an actively functioning E-Cell. It's a 5-6 month-long competition where we guide students by giving them tasks that are essential for any Entrepreneurship cell to work smoothly.
